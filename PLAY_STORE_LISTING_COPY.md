## Full Description
Meet Pomodoom, a fun and focused productivity timer built to help you stop procrastinating and actually finish what matters.

Pomodoom combines classic Pomodoro timing with playful pressure. You choose your focus and break lengths, then pick a penalty style that matches your discipline level.

Why Pomodoom works:
- Focus timer that keeps sessions structured and simple
- Flexible break and long-break settings
- Penalty modes that add accountability when you leave focus mode
- Helpful task list to keep your current priorities visible
- Session tracking and daily progress stats
- Sound and vibration feedback so you never miss a transition
- Friendly onboarding and tutorial replay anytime

Purpose and goal:
Pomodoom is designed to make deep work easier and distraction harder. The goal is to help you build consistent study and work habits through short, repeatable sessions with clear consequences for interruptions.

Who it is for:
- Students preparing for exams or assignments
- Professionals doing deep work blocks
- Anyone who wants a focus routine that feels more like a game and less like a chore

No ads. No clutter. Just focused momentum, one session at a time.

Use Pomodoom to turn intention into action and make your productive streaks stick.
