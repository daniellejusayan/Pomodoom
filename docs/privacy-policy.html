Overview

Pomodoom is a productivity and focus timer app. We store your app data locally on your device to provide timer, task, and progress features.

Data We Process

  • Focus and break settings
  • Session history (start time, end time, duration, optional notes)
  • To-do task entries and completion status
  • Onboarding and tutorial completion flags
  • App usage counters used for statistics

How Data Is Used

  • Run focus sessions and break cycles
  • Show your personal progress statistics
  • Save your preferences
  • Restore your tasks and settings between app launches

Storage and Sharing

  • Data is stored locally using on-device storage.
  • Pomodoom does not sell your data.
  • Pomodoom does not share your local data with advertisers.
  • Pomodoom has no ads SDK in this release.

Firebase Services

Pomodoom includes Firebase configuration for app infrastructure. In this release, native analytics initialization is disabled. If cloud-backed features are added in future versions, this policy will be updated before release.

Data Retention and Deletion

Data remains on your device until you remove it. You can clear all local data from the app by using: Settings > Data Controls > Clear All Data. You can also uninstall the app to remove local data.

Children's Privacy

Pomodoom is intended for users age 13 and above.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect app data. However, no method of electronic storage is perfectly secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when app features change. The updated version will include a new Last updated date.
